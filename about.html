Our Story

FirstFix Interiors started as a one-man operation out of Hurlstone Park. Over two and a half decades, we've grown into a trusted name across the Inner West and greater Sydney — but we've never lost the hands-on approach that built our reputation.

We specialise in decorative plastering across all property types: Federation terraces, Californian bungalows, Art Deco apartments, and modern new builds. Whether it's restoring original cornices on a heritage-listed home or laying a smooth contemporary finish, we bring the same care to every job.

We're fully licensed and insured in NSW, and every quote comes directly from the owner — no middlemen, no surprises.
