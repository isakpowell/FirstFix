About This Project

Premium plasterwork for a modern hospitality space

The Leichhardt Hotel required a full plaster fit-out for its gaming lounge renovation. The brief called for a clean, contemporary interior finish — smooth set walls and curved partition elements that would complement the room's modern joinery and lighting scheme.

Commercial hospitality work demands a higher degree of precision than standard residential jobs. Surfaces need to be perfectly flat and blemish-free to stand up to direct lighting at all angles — any imperfection becomes visible under the LED strips used throughout modern gaming rooms. We applied a multi-coat smooth set finish, working the curved partition profiles to a consistent radius throughout.

The completed plasterwork provides a clean, bright canvas that ties together the room's fit-out elements — from the timber veneer cabinetry to the recessed ceiling features — delivering the seamless, high-quality finish expected in a commercial hospitality environment.

Scope of Work

  • check_circle Full smooth set plaster finish to all walls throughout the gaming lounge
  • check_circle Curved partition walls plastered to a consistent radius profile
  • check_circle Multi-coat application achieving a flat, blemish-free surface under direct lighting
  • check_circle Integration around recessed lighting, HVAC vents, and electrical penetrations
  • check_circle Final finish left paint-ready to commercial specification
